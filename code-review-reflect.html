Reflection and Synthesis: Code Review

From this code review, we received great feedback overall. Some of the key points that were noted consist of the following:

  1. Fading to an equilibrium while transitioning from one song to another
  2. Needing to complete a measure before transitioning to the next
  3. A point of contact to consider is the DJ working on Saturday
  4. We don't necessarily need to match beats Given this feedback, our next step is to look more into finding the chorus of a song and fading during the measures following the end of a chorus.

Our review itself wasn't perfect, but preparing for it was difficult because this is the first time our team has all been together. We have already experienced miscommunication; however, we feel that today went fairly smoothly and our most important questions were answered. We have a clearer direction to head in, and will continue to use Echonest. Looking at our Powerpoint, some of our slides were redundant and we probably didn't need as many as we did, but outlining our code architecture was helpful.
